## Blocker Escalation

When a task is blocked >1 day:

1. Document in Weekly Update
2. Assign owner to resolve
3. Set resolution ETA
4. Escalate to tech lead if needed
5. Daily standup on blocker
6. Remove blocker or reprioritize

## Questions to Ask Weekly

1. **Progress**: Are we on track to complete the phase by the target date?
2. **Quality**: Is the quality meeting our standards (>90% test coverage)?
3. **Blockers**: Are there any blockers preventing progress?
4. **Velocity**: Are we maintaining consistent velocity across sprints?
5. **Skills**: Does the team have the skills needed for upcoming tasks?
6. **Communication**: Is the team informed and aligned?
7. **Risks**: Are there emerging risks to the timeline?
